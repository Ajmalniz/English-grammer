## Quick Reference: Common Patterns

### Tense Indicators
- **Present Perfect:** for, since, already, yet, just
- **Past Perfect:** before, after, by the time
- **Present Continuous:** now, at the moment, currently
- **Past Continuous:** when, while, at that time

### Preposition Patterns
- good **at**
- interested **in**
- arrive **at** (place), arrive **in** (city/country)
- depend **on**
- listen **to**

### Common Errors
- Between you and **me** (not "I")
- She is good **at** (not "in")
- I **don't have any** (not "no")
- **Each** student has (not "have")
